What To Do Next

If your number is NOT registered:

Go to the official source. The only place to register your number for free is the Federal Trade Commission's website.

Official Registration Website: https://www.donotcall.gov

If your number IS registered but you still get calls:

This is common. The DNC list stops sales calls from legitimate organizations. It does not stop:

  • Political calls
  • Charities
  • Surveys

You can file a complaint with the FTC at https://reportfraud.ftc.gov.

For more information:

All official information and resources can be found on the FTC's website: donotcall.gov. They are the definitive source for all rules and regulations.
